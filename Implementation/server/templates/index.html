Face

The detection of the face is an essential feature for this monitoring system. Though this detection could serve as an attendance recording procedure at the initial stage of the monitoring process, the availability of the student through out the monitoring session is highly important. The detection of multiple faces comes to play.

This system calculates a good estimation of the angle of orientation of the face of a student. The system utilizes 3D Coordinate Geometry to achieve this estimation.
